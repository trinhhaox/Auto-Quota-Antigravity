# AG Manager: The Ultimate Automation & Monitoring Suite 🚀

**AG Manager** is a premium, secure extension for **Antigravity IDE**, designed to streamline your development workflow through intelligent automation and real-time insights.

---

## 🌟 Key Pillars

### 1. 🛡️ Absolute Security (Privacy First)
Developed with an **Anonymous** approach, AG Manager ensures your safety in the Antigravity ecosystem:
- **Local-Only**: No data collection, no external telemetry.
- **Transparent**: Clean, optimized code that respects developer privacy.
- **Trusted**: Built to replace untrusted community extensions.

### 2. 🎯 Intelligent Automation (v1.1.0+)
Save thousands of clicks every day with our new automation engine:
- **Auto-Click Approval**: Automatically handles prompts like `Run`, `Always Allow`, and `Accept`.
- **Smart Wait**: Automatically dismisses "Keep Waiting" dialogs on Windows.
- **Auto-Scroll**: Keeps your chat panel perfectly focused on the latest AI responses.

### 3. 📊 Real-Time Quota Dashboard
Never run out of tokens mid-task:
- **Visual Gauges**: High-fidelity progress bars for Gemini, Claude, and GPT models.
- **Precision Timers**: Accurate "Refreshes in..." countdowns synced directly with the IDE.
- **Status Bar HUD**: Your most critical quotas, visible at a glance.

---

## 📦 Quick Start

1.  Open Antigravity IDE.
2.  Press `Ctrl + Shift + P` and search for **"Install from VSIX..."**.
3.  Select `ag-manager-1.1.0.vsix`.
4.  Click the **Dashboard icon** in the activity bar to start.
